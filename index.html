About Me

During high school I attended a concurrent course during my last two years where I learned programming for half of the day at a place called Jordan Applied Technology Center. I have attempted college a couple of times but life ultimately overcame my attempts and I did not ever complete my first year.

I have had a varried number of jobs so far in my life.
